debut EP out now

So I feel like I've been gearing up to this moment for the last 10 years and can finally tick this off my bucket list...

I released my first music!

Finding the Seed to Stage community this time last year was such a huge turning point for me, and everything just snowballed since then in the best way. Being at the Asheville academy and meeting people in 3D made me realise I am a real musician and my music is worth exploring and developing, which was the catalyst I needed to get to the finish line with this (more on that here). It truly changed my life and set me on an exciting new creative trajectory - I know I'll be riding that wave for years to come.

Everyone below has been instrumental in the release of this record: either by providing feedback or giving me opportunities to learn and grow as an artist. This wouldn't have happened without your support and encouragement.

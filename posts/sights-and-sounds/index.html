sights and sounds project

Crowdsourcing sounds

I wanted to find a way to honour these ideas that really drove this project and have left a lasting impression on me:

  • Community care and sharing in learning and creating art
  • Contributing ideas to inspiring each other
  • Setting aside control and working intentionally with given artefacts

While working on the album, I was learning about Web3 technology, blockchain programming and NFTs. I was reading about interesting things other artists are doing in this space.

I find the overlap of technology and art really exciting, and I’ve always enjoyed easter eggs and diving deeper into the artistic works I really like.

I’m excited to explore new ways that artists can fund and promote their creative practice, so I’m taking my learnings above into a Web3 project.

With the release of the 28 tracks comes the release of 28 corresponding NFTs.

Buying an NFT gives you the opportunity to submit a sound to be used on a future release. If you trade the NFT then the recipient also has this opportunity. You’ll be credited on the release and I’ll send you an email with details on how your sound made it into the release.

How do I buy the NFTs?

It’s simpler than you might think:

  1. Get a crypto wallet such as MetaMask (free)
  2. Use MetaMask to buy some ETH - this is the cryptocurrency you’ll use to buy the NFT. You’ll need at least 0.005 ETH.
  3. On OpenSea, choose one of the album tracks to buy, and click “Buy”

Once you complete the process, you should see the NFT in your MetaMask wallet like this:

How to contribute your sample

When you own one of these NFTs, it gives you the right to log onto a special website and contribute your audio recording to be used in one of my future releases. Only NFT holders are able to do this.

Head to 3xx.thisisrooms.com and click “Authenticate With Crypto Wallet.” You’ll be asked to authenticate with MetaMask, and MetaMask will tell the website whether or not you have one of the #3xx NFTs in your wallet. If you do, you’ll be invited to upload your sample.

Can I trade the NFT with someone else?

Yes! If you trade the NFT with someone else, they will be able to login to the website above and contribute a sample. In this way, an infinite amount of samples can be contributed if people keep trading the NFTs.

Bear in mind that if you trade your NFT with someone else, you will no longer own it. There will only ever be a maximum of 28 NFT holders at any one time.

Also note that if you trade your NFT with someone else and they trade it back to you, you won’t be able to upload a second sample. However, if someone trades a different track NFT with you, you will be able to upload a new sample.

Basically you can upload one sample per NFT you own. So if you own 5 of the NFTs, you can upload 5 samples, even if you trade them away and back again.

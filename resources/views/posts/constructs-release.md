# constructs EP out now

![constructs cover art](/images/releases/constructs-cover.jpg)

I'm excited to announce my second EP is out now!

It's quite a departure from my first EP and was a lot of fun to make, even though it's taken SO long to finish this one and countless versions of each track...

Thank you to [Circul & Eas](https://circulandeas.com/) for your critical listening and feedback nearing the end of the process.

Thank you to [Earthcry](https://linktr.ee/earthcry) for your continued tutorship and support through the Seed to Stage academy.

Thanks to [Enigmada](https://lnk.bio/enigmada) for listening to an early version of razer and helping me come to terms with the fact that I needed to completely overhaul it... also for your continued music theory and sound design support.

Thank you to [Incertezza](https://linktr.ee/incertezza) for contributing your amazing sound design skills on aurora.

Thank you to [Loïc Valenti](https://linktr.ee/loicvalenti) for your mixing feedback and artful mastering.

Thank you to [Emily Triplett](https://www.instagram.com/emtriplett.art) for the incredible artwork for this release. It was so much fun and so rewarding working with you on this to produce something so unique and fitting with the concept for the album.

Thank you to my boyfriend for your unending curiosity for my art and continued encouragement to keep at it.

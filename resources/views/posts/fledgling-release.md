# debut EP out now

![fledgling cover art](/images/releases/fledgling-cover.jpg)

So I feel like I've been gearing up to this moment for the last 10 years and can finally tick this off my bucket list...

I released my first music!

Finding the [Seed to Stage](https://seedtostage.com/) community this time last year was such a huge turning point for me, and everything just snowballed since then in the best way. Being at the Asheville academy and meeting people in 3D made me realise I am a real musician and my music is worth exploring and developing, which was the catalyst I needed to get to the finish line with this ([more on that here](https://rachelconnor.substack.com/p/episode-4-stories-of-creative-courage)). It truly changed my life and set me on an exciting new creative trajectory - I know I'll be riding that wave for years to come.
